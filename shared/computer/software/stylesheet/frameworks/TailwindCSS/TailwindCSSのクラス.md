## Tier 1

- 要素の間隔 (p, m)
- 文字や背景の色 (text-{color}, bg-{color})
- 文字の大きさや太さ (text-{size}, text-base, font-{weight})
- 文字の位置 (text-center)
- flex系 (flex, flex-col, flex-wrap, items-center, justify-between, justify-center, flex-1, shrink-1, gap)
- 寸法 (h-full, w-full, h, w, max-w, min-w, size)

## Tier 2

- 枠線 (border)
- border-radius系 (rounded)
- 要素間の余白 (space)
- flexとgrid以外のdisplay系 (block, inline, inline-block, inline-flex)
- visibility系 (hidden, invisible)
- position系 (relative, absolute, top, left, inset)
- overflow系 (overflow-hidden, z)
- hover設定 (hover:)
- focus設定 (focus:)
- レスポンシブ設定 ({size}:)

## Tier 3

- grid系 (grid, grid-cols, col-span, grid-rows, place-items-center, gap)
- カーソル (cursor)
- box-shadow系 (shadow)
- 長文の処理 (truncate, whitespace-nowrap, line-clamp, break-words)
- pointer系 (pointer-events-none, select-none)

- その他
